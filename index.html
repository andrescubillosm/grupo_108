About Eficacia

.

Eficacia SA is a Colombian company with 37 years of experience in business solutions, brand care, retail operation and human management processes. The company has a presence in more than 800 municipalities in the country. It is headquartered in the city of Cali, Valle del Cauca region and was established on August 12, 1991.
